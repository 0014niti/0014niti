Core Features & USPs

✨
Magical Auto-Beautify

Write naturally with your stylus or finger. Nnotes' intelligent engine automatically transforms messy handwriting into perfectly styled digital typography in real time or via Lasso selection.

🌌
Limitless Infinite Canvas

Break free from page restrictions. Pan and pinch-zoom endlessly across a limitless canvas with customizable Dot, Ruled, Graph, and Plain background grids.

🎨
Claymorphism 3D UI

Experience a soft 3D 'Claymorphism' user interface that is easy on the eyes and deeply tactile to use, supporting seamless Light & Dark workspace themes.

🧰
Floating Drag & Drop Palette

A customizable floating toolbar that stays out of your way. Move it anywhere on screen, long-press to reorder tools, or switch between vertical, horizontal, and split layouts.

🩺
3D Interactive Mascots

Choose a profession-based 3D companion (Doctor, Student, Engineer, Artist) that hangs out on your toolbar and provides motivational boosts during long study sessions.

🛡️
Local-First SAF Privacy

No accounts required, no cloud tracking, zero telemetry. Files are managed locally using Android's Storage Access Framework for 100% data ownership.
