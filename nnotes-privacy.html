Privacy Policy • Section 1

1. Privacy Philosophy & Commitment

At Nnotes, we believe that your thoughts, notes, and collaborative documents belong solely to you. Designed and engineered by Dr.N (Niti) and Harshita Doddagoudar, Nnotes is built with a local-first, privacy-respecting architecture.

🔒
Zero Monetization of Personal Data: We do not track your behavior, sell your personal information, or monetize your note data to advertisers or third-party brokers.
Privacy Policy • Section 2

2. Information We Collect (and Don't Collect)

Nnotes strictly limits data processing to what is functionally required for note-taking and peer synchronization:

  • Note Contents & Attachments: Stored locally on your device. We do not store or read your note content on remote central servers unless you explicitly trigger collaborative cloud sync services.
  • No Personal Identity Tracking: You can create and edit notes without revealing your real identity, email, or telephone number.
  • Diagnostics & Crash Reports (Optional): Minimal, non-identifiable error logs (such as OS version and memory allocation stack traces from our C-engine) may be processed strictly for bug fixing.
Privacy Policy • Section 3

3. Storage, C Engine & Memory Protection

Nnotes utilizes a high-performance C core for lightning-fast text processing and Kotlin for Android integration. Data stored locally on device benefits from:

  • AES-256 local database encryption for sensitive categories.
  • Automated memory wiping of transient note buffers in the C engine upon closing notes.
  • Sandboxed Android application storage isolating Nnotes data from other malicious software.
Privacy Policy • Section 4

4. Real-Time Sync & Peer-to-Peer Collaboration

When using Nnotes' collaboration features to work live with teammates, data payload packets are transmitted securely using TLS 1.3 encryption.

⚡
Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Security: Collaborative sessions prioritize direct socket connections between authorized peers. No unauthorized central server maintains persistent access to your active session streams.
Privacy Policy • Section 5

5. Android & System Permissions

Nnotes requests only essential device permissions on Android and desktop platforms:

  • Storage / Media: Required to save exported notes (.txt, .md, PDF) and load custom media attachments.
  • Network Access: Required strictly for real-time note synchronization and peer collaboration when enabled.
  • Notifications: Optional permission to display note reminders and collaboration invites.
Privacy Policy • Section 6

6. Your Data Control & Data Wiping

Because your notes are stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data:

  • Export: You can export your entire library at any time into standard open formats (JSON, Markdown, Plain Text).
  • Immediate Data Erasure: Clearing app storage or uninstalling the app permanently purges all local notes from device memory.
Terms of Service • Section 7

7. Terms of Service & Acceptable Use

By using the Nnotes application, you agree to comply with the following conditions:

  • You will not use Nnotes to transmit illegal content, malicious scripts, or attempt to compromise peer devices during sync sessions.
  • You will not reverse engineer or exploit collaboration socket protocols for unauthorized telemetry collection.
