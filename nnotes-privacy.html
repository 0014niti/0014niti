Privacy Policy • Section 1

1. Privacy Philosophy & Local-First Design

Nnotes is engineered by Dr.N (Niti) and Harshita Doddagoudar around a strict local-first, private-by-default model. We believe your handwritten notes, mathematical derivations, medical diagrams, and annotated PDFs contain sensitive personal intellectual property that should never be monitored, mined, or uploaded without your explicit command.

🔒
No Account Requirement & No Tracking: Nnotes requires no user login, no cloud profile creation, and includes zero tracking SDKs, analytics, or advertising frameworks.
Privacy Policy • Section 2

2. Zero Telemetry & Data Collection

When you use Nnotes on Android, your data processing is entirely self-contained on your device:

  • Note Vector Data (.nnote): Every pen stroke, highlighter mark, and vector shape remains stored locally on your device storage.
  • No Server Analytics: We do not log your stroke metrics, stroke count, document titles, or active session duration.
  • No Third-Party Analytics: No Google Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, or ad identifiers are linked or embedded in Nnotes binaries.
Privacy Policy • Section 3

3. Local File Storage & Android SAF Isolation

Nnotes integrates directly with Android's Storage Access Framework (SAF). This privacy feature ensures:

  • Zero Broad Storage Permissions: Nnotes does not require `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` or `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` broad access to your photo library or personal folders.
  • User-Granted Directory Isolation: Files are read and written strictly in directories you select via the system document picker.
  • Unflattened Vector Precision: Notes saved in vector formats retain full editability without flattening stroke data into permanent raster bitmaps.
Privacy Policy • Section 4

4. C/C++ Native Engine & Memory Safety

Nnotes utilizes a custom native C/C++ rendering pipeline (compiled with Android NDK) for off-thread deep zoom, high-resolution stroke interpolation, and live multiply blending:

⚡
Memory Buffer Recycling: Off-screen viewports and stroke tree buffers generated during pan & zoom actions are discarded from RAM immediately upon redrawing, preventing memory leakage and unauthorized cache persistence.
Privacy Policy • Section 5

5. PDF Annotation & On-Device Color Inversion

When annotating PDFs or applying Nnotes' smart night-mode PDF color inversion:

  • PDF parsing and rendering are processed 100% on-device using optimized local rendering libraries (PDFBox Android).
  • PDF files opened in Nnotes are never transmitted to remote OCR, conversion, or preview servers.
Privacy Policy • Section 6

6. Network Permissions & Presentation Server

Nnotes includes `android.permission.INTERNET` in its manifest strictly for optional features:

  • Local Presentation Server: Allows mirroring active note canvases over your local Wi-Fi network onto external displays or desktop companion browsers.
  • No External Phone-Home Connection: The network layer does not establish persistent background connections to central external servers.
